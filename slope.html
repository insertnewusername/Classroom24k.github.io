About the Game

Slope is a fast-paced 3D endless runner game where you control a ball rolling down a randomized, neon-lit track. Drive your ball to follow the straight line in space and avoid obstacles as they crash through the race in Slope.

Controls

  • A / D or Left / Right Arrows: Steer Ball
  • Enter: Restart Game
  • Esc: Exit Fullscreen

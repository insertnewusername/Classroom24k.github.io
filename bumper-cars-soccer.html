Bumper Cars Soccer

About the Game

Bumper Cars Soccer is a high-speed physics game. Drive your bumper car, hit the giant soccer ball, and score goals to win!

Controls

  • WASD / Arrows: Drive and Steer
  • Spacebar: Nitro Boost
  • R Key: Reset Car

Pro Tip

Use your Nitro Boost right before hitting the ball to send it flying with much more power!

Bumper Cars Soccer

About the Game

Bumper Cars Soccer is a high-octane physics game where you drive a sturdy bumper car to strike a giant soccer ball into the opponent's goal. Use your speed and weight to outmaneuver the other cars!

Controls

  • WASD or Arrow Keys: Drive and Steer your car.
  • Spacebar: Activate Nitro Boost for extra power.
  • R Key: Reset your car if you get stuck or flipped.

Tips for Winning

Don't just chase the ball! Try to time your Nitro Boost right before you hit the ball to send it flying across the arena. Defending your own goal is just as important as attacking.

Retro Bowl

About the Game

Retro Bowl is a fun, 8-bit-style American football game. It is a simple, fast paced arcade gameplay with team management strategy.

Controls

  • WASD: Movement
  • Spacebar: Jump
  • Shift: Sprint
  • C: Crouch
  • B / E: Shop & Inventory
  • Esc: Exit Fullscreen / Cursor Lock

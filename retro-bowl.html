About the Game

Retro Bowl is the perfect game for the armchair quarterback to finally prove a point. Can you manage your roster, handle the press, and lead your team to the ultimate prize?

Controls

  • Mouse / Touch: Aim and Throw
  • WASD / Arrows: Dodge and Dive
  • Spacebar: Start Play
  • Esc: Pause

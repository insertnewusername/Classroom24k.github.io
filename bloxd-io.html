Bloxd.io

About the Game

Bloxd.io is a massive multiplayer parkour and building game. Note: This game is being loaded through a Google Proxy to ensure better connectivity and performance on restricted networks.

Controls

  • WASD: Movement
  • Spacebar: Jump
  • Shift: Sprint
  • C: Crouch
  • B / E: Shop & Inventory

About the Game

Bloxd.io is a massive multiplayer parkour and building game. An adventure-themed Minecraft-style game, it offers endless creative and competitive modes that are entertaining for everyone.

Controls

  • WASD: Movement
  • Spacebar: Jump
  • Shift: Sprint
  • C: Crouch
  • B / E: Shop & Inventory
  • Esc: Exit Fullscreen / Cursor Lock

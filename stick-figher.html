About the Game

Stick fighter is a fighting game, where you can choose one of the six characters, each with a brand new moveset. Fight your way through in singleplayer mode, or challenge a friend to a duel in multiplayer.

Controls

  • A/D or Left arrow keys: Move & Jump
  • A A or double left key: Attack 1
  • D D or double right key: Attack 2
  • A D or left and right key: Double Jump 1
  • D A or right and left key: Double Jump 2
  • (While Double Jumping) A or left key Attack 3
  • (While Double Jumping) D or right key Attack 4

About the Game

Experience high-octane physics mayhem! Drive your car, smash into the ball, and score goals against your opponents. Use your nitro boost wisely to score even more points.

Controls

  • WASD / Arrow Keys: Drive & Steer
  • Shift: Nitro Boost
  • R: Reset Position
  • Esc: Exit Fullscreen

About the Game

Smash Karts is a high-speed, 3D multiplayer kart battle arena game. Drive fast, pick up power-ups, and use rockets and machine guns to blast your opponents. Customize your character and prove you're the best in the arena!

Controls

  • WASD / Arrow Keys: Drive & Steer
  • Spacebar: Fire Weapon
  • Esc: Exit Fullscreen

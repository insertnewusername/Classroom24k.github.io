About the Game

Rowdy City Wrestling is a retro-style, arcade wrestling game. Experience the journey of a wrestler from the streets to the championship. The main game mode is the career mode where you fight in 1vs1 matches, royal rumble events, and a 6-person free-for-all brawl mode.

Controls

  • A / D: Move Left & Right
  • W: Jump / Climb
  • J: Punch
  • K: Kick / Dropkick
  • Esc: Exit Fullscreen
